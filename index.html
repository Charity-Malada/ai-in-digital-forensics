// 01 — About the Project
Project Mission

This Honours project investigates how Artificial Intelligence (AI) can be applied to the field of Digital Forensics. As digital crime continues to evolve in scale and complexity, traditional forensic methodologies face significant limitations in processing large volumes of digital evidence at speed. This research aims to identify, analyse, and evaluate AI-driven approaches that can automate evidence triage, improve anomaly detection, and support forensic investigators in making faster and more accurate decisions. The project is conducted under the supervision of the University of the Western Cape's Computer Science department.
